# Board Technology Brief Template

## Purpose

Provide a concise board-level format for communicating technology posture, risk, decisions, and recommended action.

## When to Use

- Quarterly board meetings
- Post-diligence board readout
- Portfolio company operating review
- Security, AI, platform, or delivery risk escalation
- Post-close or post-investment 100-day progress update

## Executive Summary

Summarize the technology posture in five to eight sentences. Focus on business relevance, material risks, leadership actions, and decisions needed.

## Business Impact

For each material risk, describe impact in business terms:

- Revenue growth
- Customer trust
- Margin or cloud cost
- Security or compliance exposure
- Acquisition integration
- Delivery predictability
- Operational resilience
- AI governance and data risk

## Management Questions

- What risks can management remediate within current budget and capacity?
- Which risks require board approval, investment, or tradeoff decisions?
- Which technology initiatives are tied directly to business objectives?
- What assumptions need validation before the next board meeting?
- What evidence should management provide at the next checkpoint?
