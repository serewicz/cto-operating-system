# 100-Day Technology Plan Framework

## Purpose

Convert diligence findings into an execution plan for CTOs, CEOs, operating partners, and boards.

## When to Use

- Immediately after investment, acquisition, or close
- After a technology diligence report identifies material findings
- During CTO transition or fractional CTO engagement
- When a company needs a board-visible technology operating plan

## Plan Types

### Growth Equity Plan

Focus: scale readiness, delivery predictability, security maturity, AI governance, platform modernization, hiring coverage, and cloud cost discipline.

Typical priorities:

- Establish technology operating cadence.
- Improve roadmap and delivery predictability.
- Reduce key-person dependency.
- Validate security controls needed for enterprise growth.
- Define AI use case governance and cost visibility.
- Prioritize platform work that supports revenue growth and customer commitments.

### Acquisition Integration Plan

Focus: knowledge transfer, identity integration, deployment standardization, documentation, support transition, vendor consolidation, and integration readiness.

Typical priorities:

- Capture critical architecture and deployment knowledge.
- Map identity, access, security boundaries, and data flows.
- Document production operations and incident response.
- Identify systems that affect integration, reporting, or customer continuity.
- Align technology roadmap with acquirer operating model.

### Turnaround Plan

Focus: immediate risk reduction, operational stability, cost control, ownership clarity, security remediation, and delivery reset.

Typical priorities:

- Stabilize production ownership and incident response.
- Review privileged access and backup restore readiness.
- Freeze or rationalize non-critical spend.
- Rebuild roadmap around capacity and business urgency.
- Assign accountable owners for critical systems and risks.

## 30/60/90/100-Day Structure

| Timeframe | Objective | Typical Work |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Days 1-30 | Stabilize and clarify | Confirm facts, assign owners, address red risks, validate backups, review access, document critical operations |
| Days 31-60 | Govern and standardize | Establish operating cadence, improve delivery process, document architecture, formalize AI/security/vendor governance |
| Days 61-90 | Modernize and scale | Begin selected platform, automation, data, AI, security, or cost initiatives tied to business outcomes |
| Days 91-100 | Board readout | Present progress, remaining risk, investment needs, decisions, and next-quarter operating plan |

## Owners

Every action should have one accountable owner. Common owners:

- CEO
- CTO
- VP Engineering
- Engineering Manager
- Product Leader
- CISO or Security Lead
- Finance
- Board
- Operating Partner

## Success Metrics

Use measurable outputs, not vague activity.

Examples:

- Backup restore test completed and documented.
- Production deployment process documented and executed by a backup operator.
- Privileged access review completed with remediation owners.
- AI use case register approved by leadership.
- Cloud cost dashboard reviewed monthly by Engineering and Finance.
- Critical systems have named primary and secondary owners.
- Board receives risk register and 30/60/90-day progress update.

## Board Checkpoints

| Checkpoint | Board Question | Evidence Requested |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 30 days | Have material red risks been validated and assigned? | Risk register, owner list, immediate remediation plan |
| 60 days | Are governance and operating cadence in place? | Monthly CTO review, ADRs, AI/security governance artifacts |
| 90 days | Are modernization and risk reduction work showing evidence of progress? | Milestone evidence, delivery metrics, cost/security updates |
| 100 days | What remains unresolved and what decisions are needed next? | Board-ready technology plan and next-quarter priorities |

## Expected Outcome

A practical execution plan that turns diligence findings into owners, deliverables, success measures, board checkpoints, and next-stage technology leadership cadence.
