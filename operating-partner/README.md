# Operating Partner Technology Advisory Frameworks

This folder contains operating partner and CTO advisory frameworks for technology diligence, board reporting, AI governance, and post-close technology planning.

These frameworks define the methodology. They are designed for use by CTOs, fractional CTOs, PE operating partners, boards, investors, and executive teams that need to evaluate technology posture, risk, leadership capacity, AI readiness, and post-close execution priorities.

Executive AI Advisor implements portions of these frameworks in software:

https://github.com/serewicz/Executive-AI-Advisor

## How to Use This Folder

- Use [Technology Due Diligence Framework](Technology-Due-Diligence-Framework.md) to structure diligence work.
- Use [Technology Risk Scoring Model](Technology-Risk-Scoring-Model.md) to make findings comparable and board-readable.
- Use [100-Day Technology Plan Framework](100-Day-Technology-Plan-Framework.md) to convert findings into execution.
- Use [Board Technology Brief Template](Board-Technology-Brief-Template.md) for executive and board communication.
- Use [AI Governance Assessment Framework](AI-Governance-Assessment-Framework.md) to evaluate AI readiness, risk, and control maturity.
- Use [Portfolio Company Technology Review](Portfolio-Company-Technology-Review.md) for quarterly operating partner reviews.

## Operating Principle

Technology diligence should not end with a list of technical observations. It should produce ownership, business impact, risk prioritization, management questions, board decisions, and an execution plan.
