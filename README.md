# CTO Operating System

Frameworks, assessments, governance models, and operating playbooks for technology leadership.

This repository exists to help leadership teams align technology decisions with business outcomes. It is a practical operating playbook for CTO-level work: clarifying ownership, exposing tradeoffs, reducing risk, scaling teams, and communicating technology strategy in terms executives and boards can act on.

## Focus Areas

- CTO leadership
- AI strategy and governance
- Kubernetes and cloud-native platforms
- Cybersecurity and technology risk
- Technology due diligence
- Platform modernization
- Engineering organization scaling
- Executive and board communication

## Who This Is For

- Founders who need a clearer technology operating model
- CEOs who need technology leadership translated into business outcomes
- CTOs who want practical frameworks for assessment, execution, and communication
- PE firms evaluating technology risk and post-close priorities
- Boards that need concise visibility into technology posture
- Technology leaders scaling teams, platforms, governance, and delivery

## How to Use This Repository

Use this repository as a working CTO playbook, not as static reading material.

1. Start with [100-Day CTO Plan](docs/100-day-cto-plan.md) when entering a new company, role, or turnaround.
2. Use [Technology Assessment](docs/technology-assessment.md) and [Engineering Org Review](docs/engineering-org-review.md) to establish current state.
3. Use [Technology Due Diligence Checklist](due-diligence/technology-due-diligence-checklist.md) for investment, acquisition, or board-level review.
4. Use [AI Governance Framework](governance/ai-governance-framework.md), [Risk Register Template](governance/risk-register-template.md), and [ADR Template](governance/architecture-decision-record-template.md) to make governance concrete.
5. Use the fractional CTO templates to drive executive cadence, board reporting, and monthly operating reviews.
6. Use the scaling templates to clarify roles, team structure, and hiring decisions.
7. Use [Operating Partner Technology Advisory Frameworks](operating-partner/) for the diligence, risk scoring, board brief, AI governance, portfolio review, and 100-day planning methodology that can be applied manually or automated in software.

## Core Operating Principle

Technology issues become business problems when leadership does not make ownership, tradeoffs, risk, and outcomes explicit.

The role of a CTO is not only to choose technologies. It is to create the operating clarity that lets the business make better decisions.
